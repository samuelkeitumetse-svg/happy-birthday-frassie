A birthday story

For Frassie

"Every story begins with turning the first page."

Chapter Two

The little girl grew.

She collected memories the way some people collect flowers. Some were beautiful. Some were messy. Some were hilarious enough to become family legends.

She discovered that growing up didn't mean losing the chaos... it just meant learning how to wear it with confidence.

And somehow... she became someone people could always laugh with, always trust, and always come home to.

Chapter Three

Some people leave footprints.

Frassie leaves... Chaos. Questionable ideas. And somehow... a trail of people smiling behind her.

Twenty years isn't measured by birthdays. It's measured by every laugh, every late-night conversation, every hug, every ridiculous story, and every person whose life became brighter because she walked into it.
