A Birthday Story

For Frassie

On your 20th Birthday

"Every story begins with turning the first page."

Chapter One

Once upon a time...

There was a little girl who insisted she was perfectly normal.

She laughed loudly. She dreamed freely. She left tiny bits of chaos wherever she went... and somehow made life more fun because of it.

Chapter Two

The little girl grew into a beautiful young woman.

Kind. Smart. Polished.

...yet somehow still a tiny chaos gremlin.

She became someone people could laugh with, trust without hesitation, make memories with, and cry with when life became too heavy.

The world became a little brighter simply because she was in it.

The Footprints We Leave

Some people leave footprints.

Frassie does too... just not in the way you'd expect.

Hers are filled with wonderfully questionable ideas... (like throwing away a burnt popcorn pot instead of washing it.)

With imaginary friends who somehow felt completely real.

With made-up bedroom games that always started with, "O batla go tshameka..."

Twenty years aren't measured only in birthdays.

They're measured by every ridiculous argument, every silly story, every laugh, every hug, and every life that became brighter because she was part of it.

Happy Birthday, Frass ❤️

Twenty years of laughter.

Twenty years of unforgettable memories.

Twenty years of making the people around you smile without even trying.

And somehow... I know the best chapters of your story are still waiting to be written.

Happy 20th Birthday.

I hope this year brings you every opportunity, every adventure, every laugh, and every happiness your heart can hold.

Love,
Big Sis ❤️

The End...
...or perhaps, just the beginning.
